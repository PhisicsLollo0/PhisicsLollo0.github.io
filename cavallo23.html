Catalogue of Open Clusters (Cavallo+2023b)

Welcome to the repository of the extensive open cluster catalogue from the study Cavallo+23b. The complete dataset is available for download below, as featured in our recent publication.

In the table cavallo23.csv are listed the 6413 clusters with the parameters estimated by our ANNs. The README file contains the description of each column of the table cavallo23.csv

Label Units Description
Cluster --- Cluster name
kind --- [omg] Type of object (o:open cluster, m:moving group, g:globular cluster) from HR23
RA deg Mean right ascension of cluster’s members
Dec deg Mean declination of cluster’s memebers
plx mas Mean parallax of cluster’s members
e_plx mas Error on the parallax
CMDclass --- 50th percentile of CMD class from HR23
quality --- Quality of the predictions (0: gold sample 1: silver sample 2:bronze sample 3:wood sample)
