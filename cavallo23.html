Catalogue of Open Clusters (Cavallo+2023b)

Welcome to the repository of the extensive open cluster catalogue from the study Cavallo+23b. The complete dataset is available for download below, as featured in our recent publication.

The included 'cavallo23.csv' file comprehensively lists 6413 clusters, detailing parameters ascertained through our advanced neural network analyses. For further insights into the dataset, the 'README' file offers a descriptive guide to the contents of the catalogue.
